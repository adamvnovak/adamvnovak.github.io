Swing Dancing

I absolutely love going swing dancing. Here's some of my favorite places I've swing danced:
  • Caveau de la Huchette in Paris, France. An underground cave with a rich history of swing dance. They host evenings dedicated to swing every Sunday night.
  • Big Band Dance at Centennial Park in Nashville, TN. All summer long the city hosts weekly evenings of music & dance.
  • Summer Concert Series at Brentwood Park in Brentwood, TN. Similar to the dances in Centennnial Park, but in a smaller, quaint suburb of Nashville. It's the most popular program the city offers all year long.
