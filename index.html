Adam Novak

I'm a student at the University of Southern California studying Computer Science & East Asian Languages.

What I'm interested in:

  • Building. I run an independent app studio called Leavey Labs. I specialize in iOS development and I'm experienced in fullstack development, ux design, and product management.
  • Language learning. I'm fluent in Korean after taking a gap year to work on farms in South Korea and self-studying for four years. I recently began learning Chinese.
  • Monastic living. I spent two summers training at Buddhist monasteries in Delaware and South Korea, and I've completed a number of 10-day silent meditation courses.
  • Responsible technology. I co-founded Shift SC, the student org which promotes socially responsible technology at USC. I've lead a number of Digital Wellbeing Workshops to help high school & college students build a healthy relationship with their personal devices. I also conducted research on web platforms and misinformation as a fellow with the USC Korean Studies Institute.
  • Hacky sack. I started hacky sack clubs at USC and Ravenwood High School to build community around the fun pasttime. I used to compete competitively.
