안녕하세요, 아답입니다~

I'm a junior at the University of Southern California studying Computer Science & pursuing fluency in Korean.

I love to stay learning, adventuring, and challenging myself. I spent a year working as an English teacher and farmhand throughout East and Southeast Asia, and I most recently lived as a monk at a Vipassana meditation center.

interests

I have broad interests and enjoy learning about and working on all sorts of things.

That being said, I'm particularly interested in the realm of human-centered and socially responsible technology. I look at how algorithms and apps can be better designed and regulated to promote human well-being and limit their negative unintended consequences on society.

projects

I'm currently serving as President of Shift SC and doing research on web platforms and misinformation with the Korean Studies Institute.

Kevin and I are also building inssajeon, the social, open source dictionary for Korean slang.
